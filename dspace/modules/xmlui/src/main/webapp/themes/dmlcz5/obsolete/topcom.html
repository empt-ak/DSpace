Kybernetika

ISSN:
0023-5954
Publisher:
Institute of Information Theory and Automation AS CR
Publication place:
Praha, Czech Republic
Journal web site:
http://www.kybernetika.cz/
Description:
Kybernetika is a bi-monthly international journal dedicated to the rapid publication of high-quality, peer-reviewed research articles in the fields covered by its title. The journal is published by the Institute of Information Theory and Automation (UTIA). Kybernetika traditionally publishes research results in the fields of Control Systems, Information Sciences, System Sciences, Statistical Decision-Making, Applied Probability Theory, Random Processes, Fuzziness and Uncertainty Theories, Operations Research and Theoretical Computer Science, as well as in topics closely related to these fields.
